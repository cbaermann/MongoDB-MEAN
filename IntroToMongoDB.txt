greater than 3

less than or equal to 10
    db.students.find({lucky_number: {$lte: 10}}).pretty()
between 1 and 9 (inclusive)
    db.students.find({ $and: [{lucky_number: {$gte:1}}, {lucky_number: {$lte:9}}]}).pretty()
